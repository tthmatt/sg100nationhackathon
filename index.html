About The Hackathon

There is no hackathon organized in Singapore specifically for the public. However, there is a huge interest among the public in coding and financial literacy. The SG100 Foundation sees the need for a hackathon and provides an outlet for the public to learn and implement their skills in projects. We aim to make this experience free and accessible for everyone. We hope that public from all walks of life can come together to enjoy this experience and benefit from the skills they learnt from this hackathon!

10 Speakers

Listen to and learn from world-class speakers and local entrepreneurs.

2 Topics

We'll tackle the issues of becoming a SmartNation and the rise of FinTech.

60 Hours

Lose yourself in the most exciting hackathon in Singapore!

200 Attendees

Create partnerships, friendships and build something amazing!
